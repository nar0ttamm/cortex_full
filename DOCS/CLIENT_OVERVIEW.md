# CortexFlow — What It Does (For Clients)

> A plain-language overview of CortexFlow for non-technical stakeholders.

---

## The Problem We Solve

When a potential customer fills out a form, visits your website, or submits an inquiry, the first few minutes are critical. Studies show that **responding within 5 minutes makes you 21× more likely to qualify that lead.** Most businesses take hours — or never respond at all — because the team is busy, unavailable, or simply overwhelmed.

CortexFlow closes that gap.

---

## What CortexFlow Does

### 1. Captures Every Lead, From Anywhere
Leads come from many places — your website, Facebook ads, Google ads, IndiaMART, Justdial, and more. CortexFlow picks them all up automatically and puts them in one clean list. No manual copy-pasting, no missed inquiries.

### 2. Instantly Notifies Your Team
The moment a new lead arrives, your team gets a **WhatsApp message and an email** with all the details. The lead also gets a warm confirmation message — so they know someone will be in touch.

### 3. AI Calls the Lead Within Minutes
Here's where CortexFlow is different. An AI assistant **actually calls the lead's phone number** — in Hindi, English, or Hinglish depending on how the person responds — and has a real conversation. It:
- Introduces itself on behalf of your business
- Asks about the lead's needs
- Answers basic questions
- Books an appointment directly if the lead is ready
- Schedules a callback if they want more time

This happens automatically, within minutes of the lead arriving, even at 2am.

### 4. Everything Is Logged and Visible
Every call, every message, every outcome is logged in the CRM. Your team can:
- Read the full conversation transcript (displayed like a chat)
- See whether the lead was interested, booked an appointment, or asked for a callback
- Add notes and follow up personally

### 5. Appointments Land Straight in the Calendar
If the AI successfully books a meeting, it goes directly into the CRM's appointments calendar. The lead gets a WhatsApp message and email confirming the date and time. Reminder messages fire automatically 24 hours and 3 hours before.

### 6. Your Team Stays in Control
Alongside the AI, your team can:
- Start a call manually from any lead's profile with one click
- Manually send appointment confirmations or callback reminders via WhatsApp/email
- Add notes, update status, view the full communications history

## How the AI Calls Sound

The AI speaks naturally in the caller's language. A typical opening sounds like:

> *"Namaste Narottam ji, main [Your Company] se baat kar raha hoon — kya aapke paas ek minute hai?"*

It then listens, responds intelligently to what the lead says, and either books an appointment or lets the lead go gracefully. It never cuts off mid-sentence and always says a proper goodbye before ending the call.

---

## What CortexFlow Is Not

- It is **not a spam caller** — it only calls people who have already expressed interest
- It is **not a replacement for your team** — it handles the first contact so your team can focus on warm, interested leads
- It does **not access any personal data** beyond what you provide
- It is **not limited to one language** — it adapts to the caller automatically

---

## Getting Started

Your workspace is already set up. Your team logs in at **crm.cortexflow.in**. From there:

1. Connect your lead sources (or import a CSV to start)
2. Your AI calling number is already configured
3. Add your WhatsApp and email credentials in **Integrations → Settings**
4. Start receiving leads and let CortexFlow handle the first call

Your team gets notified for every new lead and every call outcome. From there, it's just follow-up — with warm, already-qualified prospects.
